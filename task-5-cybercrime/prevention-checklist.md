**Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist**



\-Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts (mix of letters, numbers, symbols). Avoid using your name or DOB.

\-Enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps for extra security.

\-Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.

\-Do not approve unknown payment requests on apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm—receiving money does NOT require a PIN.

\-Avoid clicking suspicious links sent via SMS, email, or WhatsApp—they may be phishing attempts.

\-Download apps only from official stores (Google Play Store / Apple App Store) to avoid malware.

\-Be cautious on public Wi-Fi (cafes, college, etc.)—avoid logging into banking or sensitive accounts.

\-Check website URLs carefully before entering personal details—look for “https://” and correct spelling.

\-Limit what you share on social media, especially personal info like phone number, address, or college ID.

\-Log out from shared/public devices and never save passwords on them.





&#x20;**If you become a victim of cybercrime:**

Report immediately at National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (for financial frauds, act quickly!)
