**Responsible Social Media Use Checklist**





\----Do’s----

Think before you post – Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.

Maintain a professional profile – Use a decent profile picture and bio, especially on platforms like LinkedIn.

Protect your privacy – Adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing personal details like address or phone number.

Verify information before sharing – Check facts to avoid spreading misinformation.

Engage positively – Support, encourage, and communicate respectfully with others online.







\----Don’ts----

Don’t post offensive or harmful content – Avoid hate speech, bullying, or inappropriate jokes.

Don’t overshare personal information – It can lead to privacy and security risks.

Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling – It can damage your reputation.

Don’t ignore digital footprint – Remember that what you post online can stay forever.

Don’t use social media excessively – Avoid addiction; balance your academic and personal life.
